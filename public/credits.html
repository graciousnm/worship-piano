This platform exists to help you grow as a worship pianist — curating the best free resources from across the internet into one structured learning path.

Video Resources

The lesson videos linked in this curriculum are publicly available on YouTube, created by various worship piano educators and content creators. This platform simply organizes and sequences these free resources into a structured learning path.

All rights and ownership of the video content remain with their respective creators. If you are a creator and would like your content removed from this curriculum, please reach out.
